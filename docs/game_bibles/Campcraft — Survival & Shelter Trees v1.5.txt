Master Firekeeper – Campfires provide healing over time and boost morale for nearby NPCs.
Supreme Forager – Foraging can yield rare legendary ingredients.
Unbreakable Will – Near-zero exhaustion in extreme weather; strong synergy with Pack Management and any Shelter picks.

Minor Keystones at Start (choose 1 of 2 when you first put any point into Shelter Tree)

Minor: Quick Pitch – Tarp Setup and lean-tos build 50% faster.

Shelter Architect – All shelters last 50% longer and give +1 rest quality.

Tier 3 (unlocked by 5 points in any Tier 2)

Master Architect – Shelters provide bonus morale and minor healing while resting.
Eternal Winter Fort – Quinzhee becomes near-permanent in cold biomes.
True Haven – Sleeping in your shelter greatly reduces Fading Meter and grants dream bonuses (synergy with Survival Enduring Soul and General Quiet Dignity).

Unlocking Rules

5 points in any Tier 1 skill → unlocks Tier 2 for that tree.
5 points in any Tier 2 skill → unlocks Tier 3 for that tree.
Points in Shared General Tree count toward thresholds.
Minor keystones chosen at tree entry. Main keystones chosen at Tier 2 and Tier 3 (synergy considered for power).
